Empowerment Programs

Eco Trio Solutions is dedicated to empowering individuals and communities through targeted programs that foster growth, sustainability, and economic development.

Rural Youth Training

Equipping rural youth with essential skills for sustainable livelihoods through vocational training and mentorship.

This program includes hands-on training in areas such as digital literacy, sustainable farming, and local entrepreneurship. Youth are mentored by experts and connected with job opportunities or seed funding.

MSME & Startup Support

Supporting small enterprises with market access, funding guidance, and mentorship.

We help startups with business registration, financial planning, investor connections, and digital growth strategies. Dedicated mentors assist at every stage of scaling operations.

Women in Agriculture

Empowering women through training, market linkages, and agri-business support.

This program helps women farmers access better tools, practices, and markets. Training in organic farming, food processing, and cooperative models improves productivity and income.

Volunteer & Internship Programs

Opportunities for youth and professionals to contribute to sustainable development.

Volunteers and interns engage in real-world impact projects including education, health awareness, green innovation, and social entrepreneurship. Certification and career guidance included.

Investor & Partnership Linkages

Connecting impactful projects with the right partners for scaling and sustainability.

We connect social enterprises and projects with investors, CSR funds, and NGOs. Support includes pitch deck reviews, due diligence, and long-term partnership facilitation.
